About Us

Our Mission

To provide a free, open resource for those who dream of going to university.

Why Did We Start PlanningU?

Here at PlanningU, we fondly remember our Grade 12 year. Or rather, we remember the stress, frustration, and confusion that we experienced when writing our university applications. Regardless of the university, every member of the team had great difficulties in finding what universities were looking for in their applications. From writing essays to online interviews, we were sick of seeing broad, meaningless advice in the form of “Just Be Yourself!” - we wanted solid, concrete, examples.
Now that we have gone through these experiences ourselves, we wish that no one has to relive the frustration we went through. Through PlanningU, we aim to streamline the university application process for both domestic and international students by providing free, open resources to help them stay on track during the transition from high school to university.
We know how important your Grade 12 year is to you. That’s why we are providing free notes, quizzes, practice tests, and study guides for several of the most competitive subjects in Ontario to help you gain a competitive edge in class.
We’re also providing detailed advice for applying to several of Canada’s top universities and their most popular programs, including:

  • The University of Toronto
  • The University of Waterloo
  • The University of Western Ontario
  • Queens’ University
  • Ontario College of Art & Design University (OCAD)

We are truly excited to see what our website can provide for you. As you push through high school and make your way to university, we hope to be on that journey with you.
