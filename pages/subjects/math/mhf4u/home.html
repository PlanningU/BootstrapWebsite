Course Overview:


MHF4U (Advanced Functions) is the prerequisite for MCV4U (Calculus and Vectors). It looks in-depth at functions and their characteristics, including: Polynomial functions, Absolute Value Functions, Rational Functions, Trigonometric Functions, Exponential/logarithmic functions, and combinations of these functions. This course also briefly introduces the concept of rates of change, the fundamental concept that leads into calculus.

Difficulty level: ★★★★☆
